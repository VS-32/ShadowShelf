LONG DESCRIPTION (paste exactly as below)
─────────────────────────────────────────

ShadowShelf is your personal Digital Mirror — a productivity and privacy extension that shows you exactly how you spend your time online, without ever sending your data anywhere.

Everything lives on your device. No account required. No cloud sync of browsing data. No telemetry. Ever.

━━ WHAT IT DOES ━━

🕐 AUTOMATIC TIME TRACKING
Silently tracks time spent on every site and categorizes it (Work, Learning, Social Media, Entertainment, and more). See your full day at a glance in the dashboard.

⚡ FOCUS SCORE
A single daily score from 0–100 that reflects how productive your browsing was. Set a daily goal and get notified the moment you hit it.

⏱ POMODORO FOCUS TIMER
Built-in timer with customizable sessions. Automatic break reminders keep you from burning out. A streak system rewards you for consistent daily sessions.

🔥 STREAKS & PERSONAL BESTS
Track your focus streak day-over-day. See your all-time records — longest streak, highest focus score, most productive day ever.

🚫 FOCUS BLOCKER
Block distracting sites during focus sessions. When your timer is running, blocked sites show a full-screen overlay. Override for 5 minutes if you truly need access.

⏰ DAILY TIME LIMITS
Set per-category limits (e.g. 30 min Social Media). Get notified at 80% and 100% of each limit.

📌 HIGHLIGHT SAVER
Select any text on any page and save it instantly. Build a personal research library without leaving your browser.

📋 CLIPBOARD TRACKER
Automatically remembers what you copy so nothing gets lost.

🌙 DAILY & WEEKLY RECAPS
Evening summary notification with your day's stats. Sunday weekly review so you can see trends across the week.

🗂 NEW TAB DASHBOARD
Every new tab shows your focus score, daily goal progress, running timer, and an inspirational quote — making your productivity status visible at a glance.

━━ YOUR PRIVACY, GUARANTEED ━━

• All browsing history, highlights, and clipboard data is stored only in Chrome's local IndexedDB on your device
• Only your settings (not browsing data) sync via Chrome Sync
• Zero network requests from the extension itself
• No third-party SDKs, analytics, or trackers
• Open source — inspect the code yourself at github.com/VS-32/ShadowShelf

━━ PERMISSIONS EXPLAINED ━━

• storage — save your data locally and sync settings
• tabs / activeTab — detect the current site for tracking
• clipboardRead — clipboard history feature
• alarms — timer, break reminders, daily recap
• notifications — timer alerts, streak milestones, goal reached
• host_permissions (<all_urls>) — track time across all sites and inject focus blocker overlay
