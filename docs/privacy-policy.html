Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

🔒 100% On-Device · Zero Telemetry · No Account Required

The short version

ShadowShelf collects nothing. Transmits nothing. Stores everything only on your device. There are no servers, no accounts, no analytics, and no third-party SDKs.

What data is stored and where

The following data is created by your use of ShadowShelf and stored only in Chrome's local IndexedDB on your device:

  • Page visit history (URL, domain, time spent, category)
  • Text highlights you save from web pages
  • Clipboard entries captured by the clipboard tracker
  • Focus timer history and session records
  • Focus streak data and personal records

This data never leaves your browser. It is never uploaded, synced to a server, or shared with any third party.

Chrome Sync

Your extension settings (such as break interval, blocked domains, category limits, and notification preferences) may sync across your devices via Chrome's built-in sync. No browsing activity, highlights, clipboard data, or timer history is included in this sync.

Network requests

ShadowShelf makes zero network requests. The extension contains no analytics libraries, no crash reporters, no A/B testing SDKs, and no external API calls of any kind.

Permissions and why they are needed

  • storage — Save your data locally and sync settings via Chrome Sync
  • tabs / activeTab — Detect the current active site for time tracking
  • clipboardRead — Clipboard history feature (only when the feature is enabled)
  • alarms — Pomodoro timer, break reminders, daily recap, and daily data pruning
  • notifications — Timer completion alerts, streak milestones, goal notifications
  • idle — Pause tracking when you step away from your computer
  • host_permissions (<all_urls>) — Track time across all websites and inject the focus blocker overlay during timer sessions

Data retention and deletion

You can configure how many days of history ShadowShelf retains (default: 30 days). You can manually prune older data or export and delete all your data at any time from the Settings tab in the dashboard. Uninstalling the extension permanently removes all locally stored data.

Children's privacy

ShadowShelf does not knowingly collect any information from anyone under the age of 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a new "Last updated" date. Since no data is collected, any changes would only reflect new features or clarifications.
